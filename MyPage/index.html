Bio

My name is Joseph Agosti. From Cumberland, MD. I graduated from Allegany High School in 2014 where I was a member of the band for all 4 years, both concert and marching band. I then started college at Allegany College of Maryland(ACM) in Cumberland. I graduated from ACM in 2016 with an Associates Degree in Applied Technical Studies and Computer Science, an Office Applications Specialist Letter of Recognition, and a General Studies Certificate. From there, I went on to Frostburg State University(FSU). Graduating in 2018 with a Bachelor's in Computer Information Systems with a minor in Computer Science.

I started my first job in 2018 as a Mystery Shopper through Market Force as an independent contractor. My next job started in 2021 as a Research Associate for RetailData, who I am still employed with. In 2022, had a brief job as a Graphic Designer for Zmedia in Frostburg, MD. After that, I started at Hamilton Relay as a Relay Operator in Oct 2022.
