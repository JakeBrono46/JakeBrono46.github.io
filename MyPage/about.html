About Me

My name is Joe Agosti, I am currently 24 years old, born and currently live in Cumberland, MD.

I attended Cresaptown Elementary School from August 2001 until June 2007. Then, I went to Braddock Middle School for the next three years from August 2007 until June 2010. Finally, I went to Allegany High School from August 2010 until May 2014.

I then attended Allegany College of Maryland (ACM) in May 2014 and graduated from ACM in the Spring and Summer of 2016.

From there, I went to Frostburg State University (FSU) starting in the Fall of 2016 and graduated in the Fall of 2018.

Since then, I have taken Continuing Education courses at ACM. As well as, teaching myself some things to keep my brain active while I look for a job. I have received a Certificate of Completion in the Excel 2016 Series in the Fall of 2019, and another for Creating Mobile Apps with HTML5 in February of 2020.

I enjoy things like watching and following sports, playing video games, and listening to music.

I have also done some volunteer work for some local areas. The Allegany County Animal Shelter, the Western Maryland Food Bank, and Allegany Nursing and Rehab Center. On top of that, I help out at Vale Summit United Methodist Church in Vale Summit, Frostburg. I help to count the church collection and Sunday School money, pack lunches for Camp Hope, I help with the ledger, and other things that some of the other attendees cannot do.
