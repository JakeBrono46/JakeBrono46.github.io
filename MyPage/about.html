About Me

My name is Joe Agosti, I am currently 24 years old, born and currently live in Cumberland, MD.

I attended Cresaptown Elementary School 2001 until 2007. Then, I went to Braddock Middle School for the next three years. Finally, I went to Allegany High School from 2010 until 2014.

I then attended Allegany College of Maryland (ACM) in May 2014 and graduated from ACM in the Spring and Summer of 2016. From there, I went to Frostburg State University (FSU) starting in the Fall of 2016 and graduated in the Fall of 2018.

Since then, I have taken Continuing Education courses at ACM where I have received two Certificates of Completion in December 2019 and February 2020.

I have tried to teach myself new things and kind my mind fresh on the things I already know how to do. I am always willing to learn new things and improve on what I already know.

In my free time; I enjoy watching and following sports, playing video games, watching YouTube videos, and listening to music.

I have also done some volunteer work for some local areas. The Allegany County Animal Shelter, the Western Maryland Food Bank, Allegany Nursing and Rehab Center, and Vale Summit United Methodist Church.
